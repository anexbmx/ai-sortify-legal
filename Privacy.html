1. Introduction

AI Sortify helps users classify, rename, organize, and manage documents using AI-assisted workflows. This Privacy Policy explains how we process personal data when you use the AI Sortify mobile app, website, and related services.

2. Data We Process

We process data to provide document classification, account access, file organization, subscription management, support, security, and compliance.
