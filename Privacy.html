4. How AI and document processing works

AI Sortify processes user-selected documents and related text to classify them, suggest file names, extract useful fields, recommend folders, and support document workflows. Depending on the feature, document content or extracted text may be sent to hosted backend services and AI providers for processing. We use this data to provide the requested feature, not to sell personal data or build advertising profiles.

Users should avoid uploading documents they are not authorized to process. If you connect Google Drive, AI Sortify acts on files and folders that you authorize the app to access.

5. Third-party services and processors

AI Sortify uses service providers to operate the app:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, storage, and backend functions.
  • Google APIs / Google Drive for sign-in and user-authorized Drive workflows.
  • Google Gemini / AI processing services for AI-assisted document classification and extraction where enabled.
  • Apple App Store for Apple sign-in and in-app purchase processing on iOS.
  • RevenueCat for subscription entitlement, purchase status, and customer center functionality.
  • Expo / app platform services for app builds, updates, and platform functionality.

These providers process data only as needed to provide, secure, and maintain the service, subject to their own terms and privacy commitments.

9. Retention
